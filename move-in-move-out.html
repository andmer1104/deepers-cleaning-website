About Our Move In/Move Out Cleaning Service

Our Move In / Move Out Cleaning service is a complete, top-to-bottom cleaning designed to fully reset a space and prepare it for its next occupant. Whether you are moving into a new home or leaving one behind, this service ensures that every area is thoroughly cleaned, sanitized, and ready for immediate use.

This is our most detailed residential service, going beyond both regular and deep cleaning by focusing on areas that are often overlooked during everyday maintenance. We clean every room with a high level of attention to detail, ensuring the property is left in a fresh, spotless, and move-in-ready condition.

Throughout the home, we carefully clean all surfaces, including walls (where applicable), baseboards, trim, doors, and window sills. Floors are fully cleaned and detailed, including corners, edges, and under accessible areas. We also clean inside storage spaces such as cabinets, drawers, closets, and shelving units to ensure they are empty, sanitized, and ready for use.

In the kitchen, we perform a full interior and exterior cleaning of all appliances, including ovens, refrigerators, and microwaves. Cabinets and drawers are wiped inside and out, countertops are sanitized, and sinks are scrubbed and polished. We remove grease, residue, and buildup to leave the kitchen in a clean, usable condition from day one.

Bathrooms receive a full deep sanitation treatment, including scrubbing showers, tubs, tile, and grout, removing soap scum and hard water buildup, and disinfecting toilets, sinks, and all fixtures. Mirrors and surfaces are polished to a streak-free finish, ensuring the entire space feels refreshed.

This service is ideal for renters looking to secure their deposit, homeowners preparing a property for sale, landlords turning over units, or anyone wanting a completely clean start in a new space. Our goal is to deliver a detailed, move-in-ready environment that meets high standards of cleanliness and presentation.

✓ Full top-to-bottom cleaning of all rooms and surfaces
✓ Interior cleaning of cabinets, drawers, closets, and shelving
✓ Interior and exterior cleaning of appliances (oven, fridge, microwave)
✓ Detailed cleaning of baseboards, trim, doors, and window sills
✓ Floor cleaning including edges, corners, and under accessible areas
✓ Kitchen deep cleaning (countertops, sinks, backsplash, and surfaces)
✓ Bathroom deep sanitization (toilets, tubs, showers, tile, and fixtures)
✓ Removal of grease, residue, soap scum, and buildup
✓ Trash removal and final property reset
✓ Preparation of space for immediate move-in or listing
