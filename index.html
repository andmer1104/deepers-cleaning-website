About Us

Deepers Cleaning is a small, family-owned cleaning company based in Richfield, MN, founded in 2019 by Angela and Ruben Mera. With over 15 years of residential cleaning experience, they proudly serve the Twin Cities and nearby communities with personalized, top-quality cleaning services that leave homes spotless and clients satisfied.

As a family-run business, Deepers Cleaning values the personal touch, treating each home with the care and attention it deserves. Their commitment to quality is evident in every detail, from their careful approach to every cleaning task to the exceptional service they provide.
