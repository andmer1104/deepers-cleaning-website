About Us

Our Mission

At Deepers Cleaning, our mission is to provide reliable, high-quality cleaning services for the Twin Cities and surrounding areas that create cleaner, healthier, and more comfortable spaces for our clients. We are committed to delivering consistent results through attention to detail, professionalism, and a strong work ethic.

We believe that a clean environment has a direct impact on well-being, productivity, and peace of mind. Whether we are servicing a home, apartment complex, or commercial space, our goal is to exceed expectations and leave every space looking refreshed, organized, and truly cared for.

Our Values

  • Quality & Attention to Detail — We take pride in delivering thorough and consistent cleaning by focusing on the details that others often overlook. Every space we clean is treated with care to ensure high standards are met every time.
  • Reliability & Consistency — Our clients can depend on us to show up on time, communicate clearly, and deliver the same level of quality with every visit. We value trust and work hard to maintain it.
  • Professionalism — We approach every job with respect, integrity, and a commitment to excellence. From how we communicate to how we clean, professionalism is at the core of everything we do.
  • Customer Satisfaction — Our clients are our priority. We listen to your needs, tailor our services accordingly, and strive to ensure you are fully satisfied with the results.

Who We Serve

Based in Richfield, MN, we proudly serve the Twin Cities and nearby communities within 50 miles. We provide cleaning services for a wide range of spaces, including:

  • Residential homes and apartments
  • Move-in and move-out properties
  • Apartment complexes and shared living spaces
  • Offices and professional workspaces
  • Restaurants and food-service environments
  • Retail stores and commercial buildings

No matter the size or type of space, we adapt our services to meet your specific needs.

Why Choose Deepers Cleaning

  • Detail-oriented and thorough cleaning approach
  • Flexible scheduling that works around your needs
  • Services tailored to both residential and commercial clients
  • Commitment to consistent, high-quality results
  • Focus on creating clean, safe, and welcoming environments

We don't just clean — we help maintain spaces that people feel comfortable living and working in every day.
