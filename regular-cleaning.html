About Our Regular Cleaning Service

Our Regular Cleaning service is designed to keep your home consistently clean, organized, and stress-free through routine maintenance. This service focuses on maintaining cleanliness in high-traffic and frequently used areas, ensuring your space always feels fresh, comfortable, and welcoming without the need for constant upkeep on your end.

We begin by thoroughly dusting all accessible surfaces throughout the home, including furniture, shelves, baseboards, window sills, décor, and light fixtures. Special attention is given to areas where dust tends to accumulate, helping improve air quality and reduce allergens. Floors are carefully cleaned based on their type — carpets and rugs are vacuumed using high-efficiency equipment, while hardwood, tile, or laminate floors are swept and mopped to remove dirt, debris, and buildup.

In the kitchen, we focus on maintaining a sanitary and visually clean space. Countertops are wiped down and disinfected, sinks are scrubbed and polished, and stovetops are cleaned to remove grease and residue. The exterior of appliances such as refrigerators, ovens, dishwashers, and microwaves are wiped down to eliminate fingerprints, smudges, and surface grime. We also clean backsplashes and ensure all surfaces are left spotless and safe for daily use.

Bathrooms are carefully cleaned and sanitized to maintain hygiene and freshness. Toilets are thoroughly disinfected inside and out, sinks and countertops are scrubbed and polished, mirrors are cleaned to a streak-free shine, and showers or tubs are cleaned to remove light soap scum and residue. Fixtures are polished to restore their appearance, and all surfaces are left sanitized and refreshed.

Throughout the home, we pay close attention to high-touch areas such as door handles, light switches, cabinet handles, and frequently used surfaces, helping reduce the spread of germs and bacteria. Trash bins are emptied, liners are replaced, and the overall space is reset to feel clean and organized.

This service is ideal for homeowners who want to maintain a consistently clean environment without the hassle of doing it themselves. Whether scheduled weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly, our Regular Cleaning ensures your home stays in excellent condition over time while preventing buildup that can lead to more intensive cleaning needs later on.

✓ Detailed dusting of all reachable surfaces, including baseboards and fixtures
✓ Professional vacuuming of carpets and area rugs
✓ Floor care tailored to surface type (sweep + mop for hard floors)
✓ Kitchen sanitization including countertops, sink, and stovetop degreasing
✓ Appliance exterior wipe-down to remove fingerprints and buildup
✓ Bathroom deep sanitation of toilets, showers, sinks, and mirrors
✓ High-touch surface disinfection to reduce germs and bacteria
✓ Complete trash removal and space reset for a refreshed feel
